Data Acquisition

The receiver antenna location picks up signals in southern and westerly direction mostly and does badly in easterly and nothernly directions. The maximum distance is around 70km, but sometimes it loses track because of geography and built-up areas in between.

Data Quality

After a summary comparision with Flightaware data, the receiver and application seem to be doing a quite good job. Sometimes the receiver sees planes that are not on Flightaware, and sometimes it misses a plane, but overall it's comparable data quality.

Data Retention and Storage

The application currently does not retain data in an archive, it's real-time display only, with no archived data.
